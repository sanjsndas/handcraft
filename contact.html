Frequently Asked Questions

🚚 How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping takes 3-7 business days within the US. Express shipping is available for 1-2 day delivery. International shipping times vary by location.
🔄 What's your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return window for most items in original condition. Custom and personalized items may have different return policies.
🎨 Can I become an artisan partner?
We're always looking for talented artisans! Email us at artisans@artisancollective.com with samples of your work and we'll be in touch.
💳 What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and gift cards. All transactions are secure and encrypted.
🏢 Do you offer wholesale pricing?
Yes! We offer wholesale pricing for qualifying retailers. Contact us at wholesale@artisancollective.com for more information.
🎁 Can I request custom orders?
Many of our artisans accept custom orders. Contact the specific artisan through their product page or email us for assistance.
